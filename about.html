About Me

Amal ~ Mardiya

I am the photographer behind photographyrosegold.uk. I believe every family deserves to hold on to their most precious moments with beauty and dignity.

My name is Mardiya, and my journey with photography began in 2018. After the passing of my father in 2021, I found it difficult to continue with the same passion for some time. Even so, I still took on wedding work in 2023 and 2024, and now in 2026, I feel ready to reconnect with my camera again, with a deeper appreciation for meaningful moments and all that photography can preserve.

My style is romantic, airy, and natural. I focus on genuine emotion, graceful details, and gentle direction so you can feel comfortable and truly present in front of the camera.

From weddings and family portraits to studio sessions and intimate milestones, my goal is to provide a calm and professional experience that feels heartfelt from start to finish.

With compassion and respect: I also offer photography sessions for families facing heartbreaking circumstances, including a seriously ill child or babies who are very unwell, nearing end of life or who have sadly passed away. These sessions are offered for a small fee to help cover travel and basic costs, and may take place in hospital, at home or another suitable location depending on each family's needs.
