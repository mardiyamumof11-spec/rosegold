Compassionate memory sessions

In some of life’s most difficult moments, photography can provide a lasting, gentle memory for families. With compassion and respect, photographyrosegold.uk also offers photography sessions for families facing heartbreaking circumstances, including a seriously ill child or babies who are very unwell, nearing end of life or who have sadly passed away.

These sessions are offered for a small fee to help cover travel and basic costs, and may take place in hospital, at home or another suitable location depending on each family’s needs.
