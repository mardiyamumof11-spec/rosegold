Compassionate memory sessions

In some of life’s most difficult moments, photography can provide a lasting, gentle memory for families. Rose Gold UK Photography offers these sessions free of charge when a family has a seriously ill child, or for babies who are unwell, at end of life, or have sadly passed away.

Sessions can take place in hospitals, at home, or in another suitable location, depending on what is most comfortable and appropriate for the family.
